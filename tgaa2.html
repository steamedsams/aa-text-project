The Great Ace Attorney 2: Resolve

大逆転裁判２ -成歩堂龍ノ介の覺悟- (Dai Gyakuten Saiban 2: Naruhodō Ryūnosuke no Kakugo) was first released in Japan on August 3, 2017 for the Nintendo 3DS. It was localized into English and released worldwide as "The Great Ace Attorney 2: Resolve," along with its predecessor, as part of "The Great Ace Attorney Chronicles" on July 27, 2021 on PC and various consoles.

A continuation of "The Great Ace Attorney: Adventures," it follows Phoenix Wright ancestor and Japanese defence attorney Ryunosuke Naruhodo at the turn of the 20th century. Now with a few cases under his belt, Ryunosuke continues to solve crimes across Victorian-era London with his trusted judicial assistant, Susato Mikotoba. When an all-too familiar face unexpectedly turns up, and investigation into a murder case starts to blend the past and the present, it's up to Ryunosuke to steel his resolve and uncover the truth before it's buried for good.
