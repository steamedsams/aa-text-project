Post-Mortem Report

午後2時すぎに死亡。肺に達する刺し傷により死亡と推定。刺し傷は1カ所。

Time of death just after 2 p.m. Death is believed to be due to trauma to the victim's lung from a knife blade. Only a single wound was identified.

Contents

被害者
氏名 : ジェゼール・ブレット
女性/英国人

死亡推定時刻
八ハチ月拾壱ジュウイチ日 午後弐ニ時頃
(検分、および目撃証言より特定)

Victim
Jezaille Brett
Female, British

Time of Death
11th August, at around 2 p.m.
(Ascertained by examination and witness statements)

死因
背中より一度、深く刺される。肺に到達する傷による失血死。
凶行から数分以内に絶命。

備考
両眼に、極度の瞳孔の収縮を認める。

Cause of Death
Single, deep stab wound from behind, piercing a lung and resulting in a fatal haemorrhage.
Death would have occurred within minutes.

Notes
Extreme miosis - pupil constriction - was observed in the victim in both eyes.

毒藥の記事

Poison Article

帝都勇盟大学ていとゆうめいだいがくの研究室から盗まれた毒薬の記事。責任者の父ですら知らなかった様子。

An article about poison that was stolen from a laboratory at Yumei University. Even my father, who is responsible for the lab, knew nothing of the incident.

帝都勇盟大学ていとゆうめいだいがくの研究室から盗まれた毒薬の記事。豆籾まめもみ記者が盗み聞きした情報で書いたもの。

An article about poison that was stolen from a laboratory at Yumei University. The reporter, Menimemo-san, based it on information he gleaned by eavesdropping.

Contents

《帝都勇盟大学医学部研究室ヨリ “劇薬” 盗マル!》


8月11日、同大学講堂にて夏目なつめ漱石そうせき氏の講演会の後、恐るべき事件が発覚した。
勇盟大学ゆうめいだいがく医学部の研究室にて極秘ごくひ開発中の猛毒が、何者かの手で盗まれたという。

Exclusive! Deadly Poison stolen from Yumei Medical Research Laboratory!


Following a lecture at Yumei by Soseki Natsume on 11th August, it's come to light that a very disturbing incident took place.
A deadly poison being secretly developed in the university's forensic science laboratory was stolen.

《少量デ死二至ル!》


この猛毒を、わずかな量でも口にする、或あるいは刃物に塗布とふして刺すことで体内に摂取されると、数分後、死に至る。
開発中の新薬ゆえ、検出は不可能であり、大学の管理体制が問われるは必至である。

- Deadly in Tiny Quantities! -


Even the smallest amount entering the body, either via the mouth or via a wound from a poison-laced blade, would prove fatal in minutes.
Current methods cannot detect the newly developed chemical. The university would have to be consulted.

《何故、毒ノ開発?》


服毒して数分、呼吸困難を発症。やがて瞳孔が針の先のように収縮して死に至る。この特徴が現れたら、死因の推定は可能。
猛毒は数種のアルカロイドから精製した新薬で、軍部の依頼で作られたとの噂も。

- Why is the Poison Being Developed? -


Onset of symptoms occurs in minutes, starting with impaired breathing and ending with acute contraction of the pupils prior to death. Such symptoms would be suggestive of this toxin. It's apparently an entirely new synthesis of alkaloids and rumoured to have been commissioned by the military.
